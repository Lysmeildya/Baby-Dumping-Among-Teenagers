What is Baby Dumping ?

Baby dumping is the act of those irresponsible human who dump their babies who are newborn or a baby younger that 12 months in the public toilet, garbage bin or even in the jungle or bushes. It has become a serious social problem in our country because the number of baby dumping cases in our country keep increasing year by year.

Majority of the people who involved in baby dumping cases are teenagers. This happens when teenagers have unprotected sex before marriage without any planninf, unreported rape cases, sex before marriage and also prostitution. After the teenagers having sex without protection, they feel very pressure when they think that the baby become a burden to them where they are not ready to take care of the baby.

Then, teenagers are unable to make a correct decision. Immature mind of the teenagers will make them to think that the best solution is to throw the baby away because they might get rejection from their parents. Some of the boy might deny their actions because they worried that people will look down on them.

There are too many factors that can lead to baby dumping cases. Literlly, baby dumping shows a bad image to the society and it will look down to our country. Teenagers can easily make mistake without the proper guidance from thr correct person.So, the government, parents and school should work together to prevent teenagers to dump their baby.
